Rules

U.S.G.A. rules and local rules shall apply (along with the following)

Rule Description
Flights & Tournament Winner Gross minus handicap equals net score (all ties decided by scorecard playoff starting with #1 handicap hole from day 2 scorecard)
Tees White Tees
Improve lie Fairway & Rough and on greens with club head only (see Below)
  1. Fairway and Rough – no closer to the hole and keep all obstacles in the line of flight.
  2. Greens – move ball sideways due to burn marks, ball marks etc.
Play ball down Traps and hazards.
Clean ball Fairway & rough and greens – replace (only applies when something foreign is visible on ball. mud etc otherwise use the Improve lie rule listed above)
Lost Ball Fairway (no penalty and drop in fairway)Rough – one stroke penalty and drop in rough Traps and Hazards (one stroke penalty and drop in trap/hazard)
Out of Bounds
  1. From same lie two O.B. max. add 4 and hit from second out.
  2. Two balls max in drink – go to safe side, add 4 & hit next shot
Putts Putt out all putts. Even the ones that are “impossible to miss”
Scoring Person that fills out the scorecard is responsible for reading scores off to scorer. Any discrepencies (addition errors etc.) can be addressed immediately.
